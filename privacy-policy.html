Privacy Policy

VidSquad is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our website and services designed to track public TikTok video performance data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or private user data from TikTok users. Our platform only processes publicly available TikTok video metadata, including:

  • Video URL
  • Number of views
  • Number of likes
  • Number of comments
  • Number of shares
  • Video description and hashtags (if publicly accessible)

We do not request, store, or access:

  • TikTok user account credentials
  • Private videos or restricted content
  • Personal identifying information (PII) of TikTok users

If you create an account on our platform, we may collect:

  • Your email address
  • Username or display name
  • Group/category preferences for organizing video data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Fetch and display analytics on public TikTok videos
  • Enable users to group videos and analyze group performance
  • Improve and maintain the functionality of our app

3. Data Storage and Security

All data collected is stored securely. We take reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of any information on our platform.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the TikTok API in accordance with TikTok's Platform Terms of Service. We do not misuse TikTok's data, and we access only data that is permitted for third-party developer use. We do not share TikTok data with external parties except as necessary to provide our service or comply with legal obligations.

5. User Rights

You may contact us at [Your Contact Email] to:

  • Request access to or deletion of your account data
  • Report concerns about how we handle data

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
